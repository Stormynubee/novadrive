1. Vision & deep analogies (memorize these)

NovaDrive is not “another maps app with hospital pins.” It is a calm emergency co-pilot that keeps working when the highway has no signal — because that is exactly when the golden hour matters most.

Analogy — The dead zone phone call:
Imagine you're hurt on NH48. You try to call 108 — one bar, then nothing. The operator asks “where are you?” and “how bad is it?” but you cannot explain lat/lng or medical terms. NovaDrive is the friend who already wrote the perfect handoff note before the call dropped.
Analogy — Wrong hospital = wrong fire station:
Sending a critical patient to the nearest clinic (no trauma surgeon) is like sending a house fire to a garden hose. Trauma-tier routing means RED patients go to trauma centers (tier 1–2), not the closest pin on the map.
Analogy — Golden Hour Packet (GHP):
Dispatchers need a story, not raw GPS. GHP is a one-screen brief: triage color, NH km marker, facility, ETA, integrity hash — like a pilot's mayday readout, readable in 10 seconds on a crackly line.
Analogy — QR human relay (sneakernet for emergencies):
Your phone has no signal; a truck driver's phone might have 4G 2 km ahead. You show a QR on your screen; they scan it; the packet lives in their phone until they can SMS 108. Like passing a sealed envelope hand-to-hand through a crowd — the network failed, the message didn't.
Analogy — LLM vs FSM (skin vs spine):
The AI chatbot is the friendly voice that understands “not breathing, can't walk.” The START FSM is the spine — deterministic medical law. The LLM never overrides triage; it only fills slots. Judges hear: “AI assists; medicine decides.”
Analogy — Calm crash dialog (no screaming app):
Panic UX kills clarity. We don't blare sirens or auto-dial 108 at second zero. We detect a possible incident, pause 15 seconds, and let the human choose. Like a co-pilot saying “possible impact — you okay?” not grabbing the wheel.

Tagline: Signal drops. The critical minute doesn't.

4. End-to-end emergency pipeline

  1. Onboarding — Splash → Guest auth → Medical profile → Accessibility
  2. Journey — Foreground GPS HUD, speed, optional CrashEngine watch
  3. SOS — Hold 1.5s or crash dialog confirm → emergency flow
  4. Locate — Capture lat/lng + NH km landmark (works offline after fix)
  5. Triage — START FSM + optional chat prefill
  6. Route — Trauma-tier filter + distance sort (not nearest clinic only)
  7. Packet — Build GHP + show QR
  8. Relay — Cache for bystander; SMS 108 when online

5. START triage FSM (medical spine)

International Simple Triage And Rapid Treatment — implemented in startTriageFSM.ts. Unit tests required; 5 tests in repo.

States: AMBULATORY → BREATHING_CHECK → AIRWAY_REPOSITION → RESPIRATORY_RATE → PERFUSION_CHECK → MENTAL_STATUS → TAGGED

6. Golden Hour Packet (GHP)

Human-readable dispatch brief — ≤800 bytes for SMS. Operators cannot decode Base64; we send plain language.

NOVADRIVE GHP
Triage: RED
Location: NH48 km 87 (13.08270, 80.27070)
Facility: Apollo Highway Trauma (~12km, ~18min)
Phone: 044-27428888
Hash: nd-a3f2c891

QR payload: minimal JSON → lz-string compress → prefix ND1: → SHA-256 integrity. If QR too large, QR carries id + triage + lat/lng + hash; full GHP stays on screen.

9. AI chatbot layer

P0: Text field “Describe the emergency” → offline parseEmergencyText on Send → pre-fills FSM slots (e.g. “not breathing”, “can't walk”). Toast: “Understood — we'll use this in triage.”

P1 (optional online): Groq/Ollama slot-fill — still FSM decides RED/YELLOW/GREEN/BLACK.

Slide line: LLM is the skin; FSM is the spine.

10. Data & SQLite POI

  id TEXT PRIMARY KEY,

  trauma_tier INTEGER, -- 1-3
  phone TEXT,

);
